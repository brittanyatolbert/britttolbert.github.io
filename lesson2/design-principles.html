design principles

BRITT TOLBERT

repetition

NETFLIX

(netflix.com)

Netflix is a great example of the principle repetition in design. Repetition is represented in colors, shapes, and typography. You will notice that all of the text, except for the logo, is in white. They use one typeface but use different fonts such as bold. White, red, and black are the main colors used. It is interesting to notice the repetition of the rectangle. It is visible in the sign in button, the email address, buttons, and even the examples of the shows in the back are organized rectangles.

white space

ROOLEE

(roolee.com)

Roolee uses great white space throughout their website. This creates a clean, simple, and navigational site. There is plenty of space between icons on the navigation bar. There is also equal white space around the website name. It also frames the picture very well. You will even notice the white space used in their icons and contact bubbles. The white space breaks up the page into sections and adds to the overall aesthetic of the site.

contrast

APPLE

(apple.com)

Apple uses bold colors, type, and photography for contrast on their websites. The first thing you notice are the contrasting black and white colors used. Threre isalso the bright blue text with the websites "call to action" letting the customer know what to do. The photo used is very close and in depth drawing you in. The colors used in the picture are similar to the website theme of dark and light.
