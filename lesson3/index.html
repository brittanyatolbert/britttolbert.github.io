weather for gardeners

This website is designed for female gardeners. Gardeners are very concious of the weather. It affects when they plant, how they water, and take care of their plants, flowers, and vegetables. This website is designed specifcally for their needs. It will be there to help you know of the effects of the weather on plants, keep you updated on the latest gardening tips, and give you reminders on planting seasons.

A huge factor is this website is its aesthetic and modern simplicity. Not only is it visually pleasing to the eye but will be easy to navigate for all your gardening needs! Welcome to your new go-to-weather site just for you!
