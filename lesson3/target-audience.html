target audience

The target audience of this website are female gardeners ages 20-60. This website will provide them with the best weather as well as tips and reminders for their plants and gardens. The purpose of this website is to help them be better at gardening. There will be reminders of good days to water, days when not to water because of precepitation, or best planting seasons. There will be articles with new developments and stories or tips and trick for them to stay updated in the garden community.


This website is meant to be the go-to weather site for anyone that gardens. This website will appeal to this target audience with bueatify simple colors that are geared toward growth, outdoors, and calmness. The typography and layout of the website will be simple and easy to navigate making hte overall experience positive for all those who use it.
