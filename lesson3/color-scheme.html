Color Scheme

Main Colors

I chose to use an analogous color scheme. I chose to use different shades of blue to represent weather. When you think of weather you think of blue. Blue is also a calming color that isn't too bold. The dark blues give contrast aginst the light and happy blues on the page. I plan to use a light blue back ground for the page.

Navigation Bar

The navigation bar will be a dark blue with light blue writing. The logo will be in the top left corner of the navigation bar.

Footer

The footer will be very similar to the navigation bar for consistency. It will also be dark blue background with the light blue text.
