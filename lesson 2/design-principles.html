Repetition

Netflix

Netflix

Netflix is a great example of the principle repetition in design. Repetition is represented in colors, shapes, and typography. You will notice that all of the text, except for the logo, is in white. They use one typeface but use different fonts such as bold.
White, red, and black are the main colors used.It is interesting to notice the repetition of the rectangle. It is visible in the sign in button, the email address, buttons, and even the examples of the shows in the back are organized rectangles.

White Space

Roolee

Roolee

Roolee uses great white space througout their website. This creates a clean, simple, and navigational site. There is plenty of space between icons on the navigation bar. There is also equal white space around the website name. It also frames the picture very well.
You will even notive the white space used in their icons and contact bubbles. The white psace breaks up the page into sections and adds to the overall design of the site.

Contrast

Apple

Apple

Apple uses bold colors, type, and photography for contrast on their websites. The first thing you notice are the contrasting black and white colors used. Threre is also the bright blue text with the websites "call to action" letting the customer know what to do.
The photo used is very colse and in depth drawing you in. The colors used in ht epicture are similar to the website theme of dark and light.
